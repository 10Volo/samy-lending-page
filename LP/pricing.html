How Does The Campaign Maker Work?

The Campaign Maker is a Facebook authorized software that connects to your existing Facebook ad account using Facebook’s marketing API to perform automated tasks with a unified goal of improving your campaign results in a quick and simple way. In short it acts as your personal Facebook marketing team at a fraction of the cost.

The Campaign Maker consists of 4 core tools:

The Creation Tool

Simplifies the A/B testing process of creating a Facebook campaign. The creation tool is our most complex and fully featured tool.

LEARN MORE ABOUT THE CREATION TOOL

The Reporting Tool

Gives you complete insight of how your Facebook marketing is going at a single glance, the reporting tool is accessible at the account, campaign, adset and ad level to see in depth data that you would otherwise miss using Facebooks’ traditional ad manager.

LEARN MORE ABOUT THE REPORTING TOOL

The Optimization Tool

Setup custom or preset rules that automatically make changes to your ads based on the ads results. This is especially helpful in limiting wasteful ads from spending too much money and limiting your losses.

LEARN MORE ABOUT THE OPTIMIZATION TOOL

The Analysis Tool

Analyzes past adset results and presents them in segmented groups so you can pin point what groups were profitable and low cost while showing you which groups you should avoid due to their high cost or low conversion rate,
